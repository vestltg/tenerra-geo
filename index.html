ANYA

A Human Continuity Operating System for special needs families. ANYA preserves what parents know about their child — observations, patterns, the texture of a life — so continuity never depends on any single person's memory across a 75+ year lifespan.

About

Vest Life, Inc. was founded by Michael Pearce — a retired special needs attorney with 30 years of IDD practice in Sacramento County, California, who spent three decades representing families of individuals with intellectual and developmental disabilities across every age and stage of life.

Tenerra is the domain under which Vest Life builds. ANYA is its first product — and the distillation of everything Michael learned listening to what special needs families actually need.
