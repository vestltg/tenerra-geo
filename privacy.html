Vest Life, Inc. | Effective Date: April 2, 2026

Sacramento, California

This Privacy Policy explains how Vest Life, Inc. ("Vest Life," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects information in connection with the ANYA platform and the ANYA AI Assistant (collectively, the "Services"). It also describes your rights with respect to your personal information.

By creating an account and using ANYA, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, do not use the Services.

This Privacy Policy is part of and should be read together with the ANYA Terms of Service. In the event of any conflict between this Privacy Policy and the Terms of Service with respect to the processing of personal information, this Privacy Policy controls.

1. Who We Are

Vest Life, Inc. is a California corporation headquartered in Sacramento, California. We operate ANYA, a cloud-based platform that allows Subscribers to build and maintain a structured record called a Vest for an individual with intellectual and/or developmental disabilities (IDD). The ANYA AI Assistant is embedded within the ANYA platform.

Vest Life is not a HIPAA Covered Entity or Business Associate.

2. Scope of This Policy - U.S. Users Only

The ANYA platform is available only to users located in the United States. The Individual for whom a Vest is created must also be located in the United States. We do not knowingly offer Services to, or process data of, individuals located outside the United States, including individuals subject to the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) or UK GDPR.

If we determine that a user is located outside the United States, their account may be suspended or terminated in accordance with the Terms of Service.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information You Provide Directly

We collect the following categories of personal information when you create an account and use ANYA:

  • Identifiers: Your name, email address, and account credentials (username and password).
  • Sensitive Personal Information (SPI): Health and disability-related information you submit as Vest Content including diagnoses, medical history, behavioral notes, therapy records, medication information, legal and guardianship documents, and other information about the Individual. This is the primary content of a Vest.
  • User-Generated Content (Vest Content): All information, files, notes, documents, and materials you enter into or attach to a Vest, including Conversations with the ANYA AI Assistant.
  • Payment Information: Billing details processed by our third-party payment processor. Vest Life does not store your full credit card or bank account information.
  • Communications: Messages you send to us via email or support channels.
  • Vest Continuity Keeper Designations: The name and contact information of any Vest Keeper you designate.

3.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use ANYA, we may automatically collect limited technical and usage information, including:

  • Log data: IP address, browser type, operating system, and access timestamps.
  • Usage data: Feature usage statistics and platform performance metrics, collected in aggregate and de-identified form.
  • Session data: Information about your activity within the platform to support security and audit logging.

We do not use cookies or tracking technologies for behavioral advertising or cross-context tracking. We do not currently respond to browser Do Not Track signals, except as required by applicable law.

3.3 Information We Do Not Collect

Vest Life does not collect or process biometric identifiers, precise geolocation data, or financial account numbers. We do not collect information from or about minors as Subscribers. Subscribers must be 18 or older.

4. The ANYA Privacy Promise

Vest Life makes the following commitments regarding your Vest Content:

  • We will not share, sell, license, or distribute your Vest Content, identified or de-identified, to any third party for any purpose without your prior express consent.
  • We will not use your Vest Content to train AI models, including the ANYA AI Assistant, or for any commercial purpose beyond delivering ANYA services to you.
  • We will not share your Vest Content with government agencies, law enforcement, or any third party except as required by a valid legal order. If compelled to disclose, we will notify you to the extent permitted by law.
  • Our cloud infrastructure partners (including Amazon Web Services / AWS) are contractually prohibited from accessing or using your Vest Content for any purpose beyond infrastructure operations necessary to run the ANYA platform.
  • In the future, Vest Life may offer Subscribers a voluntary opportunity to contribute anonymized Vest Content for AI research or training purposes. Any such program will require separate, explicit opt-in consent and will never be a condition of your subscription.

These commitments apply to your Vest Content specifically. They do not limit Vest Life's ability to use aggregate, de-identified information about how users interact with the platform (such as feature usage statistics and performance metrics) to maintain, secure, and improve the ANYA platform.

5. How We Use Your Information

We process your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To operate and deliver the ANYA platform and the ANYA AI Assistant to you.
  • To authenticate your identity and maintain the security of your account.
  • To process subscription payments and communicate with you about billing.
  • To respond to your support requests and communications.
  • To maintain, secure, and improve the ANYA platform using aggregate, de-identified usage data.
  • To fulfill legal obligations, including compliance with applicable law, responding to valid legal orders, and notifying you of data breaches as required by law.
  • To communicate with you about material changes to our Terms of Service, this Privacy Policy, or your subscription.

Sensitive personal information including health and disability-related Vest Content is used solely to deliver the services you have requested. It is not used for profiling, cross-context behavioral advertising, or any secondary commercial purpose.

6. How the ANYA AI Assistant Processes Your Information

The ANYA AI Assistant processes your Vest Content to generate responses and assist you in organizing, understanding, and acting on information about the Individual. The ANYA AI Assistant operates on AWS infrastructure using large language model (LLM) technology.

Your Vest Content is processed within your account session context. It is not used to train the ANYA AI Assistant or any other AI model, and it is not shared with other Subscribers.

The ANYA AI Assistant is an AI-powered tool that uses probabilistic methods to generate outputs based on patterns in data. The ANYA AI Assistant's outputs may be incomplete, inaccurate, or not tailored to your specific situation. The ANYA AI Assistant's responses do not constitute professional legal, medical, financial, or therapeutic advice. You should independently review and verify the ANYA AI Assistant's outputs before relying on them for any decision.

The ANYA platform and the ANYA AI Assistant are not designed for crisis intervention or real-time medical emergencies. In an emergency, contact 9-1-1 immediately.

7. How We Share Your Information

Vest Life does not sell or share your personal information as those terms are defined under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA). We do not engage in cross-context behavioral advertising.

We may share limited personal information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We share information with third-party service providers that process data on our behalf under written agreements, including AWS (cloud infrastructure and hosting) and our payment processor. These providers are prohibited from using your information for their own purposes.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose information as required by a valid court order, subpoena, or applicable law. Where legally permitted, we will notify you before complying.
  • Vest Keeper Transfers: If you have designated a Vest Continuity Keeper and a valid transfer request is received, we may provide the Vest Keeper access to your Vest in accordance with the Terms of Service and our Vest Keeper framework.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to a successor entity. Any successor will be required to assume Vest Life's obligations under this Privacy Policy and the Privacy Promise.
  • Safety: If we have a good-faith belief that disclosure is necessary to prevent imminent harm to a person's safety, we may share information with appropriate authorities. We will limit any such disclosure to what is necessary.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, subject to legal retention obligations.

  • Active Accounts: We retain your account information and Vest Content for the duration of your subscription.
  • Account Closure / Deletion Request: Upon account closure, you (or your authorized Vest Keeper) may request a complete export of your Vest Content in a portable format (JSON and/or PDF) within 30 days of the closure request. Vest Life will permanently delete all copies of your Vest Content within a reasonable time, not to exceed 180 days, after export completion (or immediately upon request if no export is requested).
  • Backup Copies: Backup copies of Vest Content may be retained for a reasonable time, not to exceed one (1) year, after permanent deletion for legal compliance and business continuity purposes, after which they are purged.
  • Account Information: Basic account identifiers (name, email) may be retained for a reasonable period after account closure for fraud prevention, legal compliance, and billing record purposes, consistent with applicable law.

9. Security

Vest Life uses reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect your Vest Content, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (TLS) and at rest.
  • Role-based access controls limiting internal access to your Vest Content.
  • Audit logging of access and activity within the platform.

No security system can be guaranteed 100% secure. In the event of a data breach affecting your personal information, Vest Life will notify you as required by applicable law, including the California Consumer Privacy Act and applicable state breach notification statutes.

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA):

  • Right to Know: You have the right to know what personal information we collect, use, disclose, and retain about you, including the categories of PI, the sources, the business purposes, and the third parties with whom we share it.
  • Right to Access: You have the right to request a copy of the specific personal information we hold about you.
  • Right to Correct: You have the right to request correction of inaccurate personal information we maintain about you.
  • Right to Delete: You have the right to request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions permitted by law (such as legal retention obligations).
  • Right to Limit Use of Sensitive Personal Information (SPI): You have the right to limit our use and disclosure of sensitive personal information to uses necessary to deliver the services you have requested. We use SPI solely for this purpose and do not use it for any secondary commercial purpose.

To submit a rights request, you may:

  • Email: support@vestlife.com
  • Self-Service: Use the privacy tools available within the ANYA platform account settings.

We will verify your identity before processing your request and will respond in accordance with applicable law, typically within 45 days. If we require additional time (up to a 45-day extension), we will notify you.

We do not sell or share personal information as defined under CCPA/CPRA, and we do not engage in cross-context behavioral advertising. Therefore, we do not offer a separate "opt out of sale" mechanism, but you may contact us if you have questions.

11. Children's Privacy

ANYA is directed to adults. Subscribers must be 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13. A Subscriber may create a Vest for a minor Individual, but only if the Subscriber is the parent, legal guardian, or otherwise has lawful authority to submit information about that minor in accordance with the Terms of Service and applicable law, including COPPA.
